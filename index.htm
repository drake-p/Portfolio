About Poiesis

... pronounced 'poy-EY-sis', from Greek  ποιησις = "making, creation, production", whence our word "poetry"

Thinking on both sides of the brain. Numbers and colors - logic and beauty - the balance of an equation and the balance of a design. I developed an analytic attitude during an intense mathematics degree and I've had a lifelong desire to be an artist. I've come to marry the two ways of thinking as a designer and coder. Motivated by perfection. Maybe perfection is unattainable, but it can be pursued. I don't care to assume it can't be found just because I haven't found it yet.
