ABOUT POIESIS

Thinking on both sides of the brain.
We are obsessed with beauty; but then, we're also obsessed with order. Unwilling to discard either the logical attitude developed during an
intense mathematical training, or a lifelong desire to be an artist, we've come to marry the two ways of thinking, for your benefit, as a designer and coder.
Motivated by perfection. Maybe perfection is unattainable, but it can be pursued. We don't care to assume it can't be found just because we haven't found it yet.

Recent projects

The Koch Curve

This spiky arrangement of equilateral triangles is the fifth step in the construction of a Koch curve fractal. The full fractal could pack a line of infinite length onto a space the size of this letter ‘l’.
